The Brush Crazy Five-Step Process

FIVE STEP PROCESS

Painting and craft projects at Brush Crazy are super fun and accessible to all of our guests in part because we had a crazy idea to create a five-step process to make sure every guest gets the very best experience with the right amount of guidance, instruction, and freedom to be uniquely creative.

1

Step One

Pick Your Project

Picking a painting project at Brush Crazy is a big decision! Your options are practically unlimited, as we offer a wide variety of painting projects and crafts. We offer freestyle canvas painting and traditional sip n’ paint style projects, and craft painting projects to include paint pouring, drip painting and more. You’ll also find shelves full of adorable blank figurines, giftable mugs, plates, and more—all waiting to be brought to life by your paintbrush

2

Step Two

Pick Your Paint

Our cheerful and friendly staff will help you follow the project guide that accompanies your chosen project, or you are welcome to choose a combination of paints and materials that help complete your personal vision. No matter your level of painting experience, our expert staff can help guide you along the process to choose the materials and tools you’ll need to complete your craft.

3

Step three

Paint Your Project

Whether you are a novice or have years of experience, Brush Crazy meets every artist at their level. When you begin painting your project, you’ll be show how to collect and organize the appropriate materials. You’ll be presented with a personalized tutorial as needed from our experienced and friendly staff to make sure you understand how to use the tools and materials for your project.
If you have any questions along the way, you can raise a hand or find one of our team members who are circulating the shop to ensure all our customers are in good artistic hands! Our staff will happily answer your questions and show you various painting techniques
4

Step Four

Let It Dry

Some types of projects—like acrylic paintings—can dry quickly and travel home with you on the same day you complete your painting craft project. However, clay crafts and other studio projects might need to be left behind for the final stages of finishing. We have a system for this! You will be issued a project ticket to accompany your project, and in the event your chosen art project needs a little extra TLC to reach that point of finished—we’ll take care of the kiln firing and you’ll receive prompt notification when it’s complete!

5

Step five

Take it Home

If your art project is a same-day-done project, you’ll be able to head home with your project in tow. We provide a box or other appropriate protection so that you may transport your finished painting project safely. In the event of clay crafts, we will notify you within 5-7 days of when your finished project is ready for pick up.
